About MT Beauty Room

Where Confidence
Begins

MT Beauty Room was founded with one simple belief: every person deserves to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin.

Located in the heart of Bovenkarspel, we are a certified beauty clinic offering a thoughtful selection of treatments — from laser hair removal and advanced facials to lash lifts and anti-ageing therapies. Each treatment is performed using professional-grade equipment and backed by years of hands-on expertise.

We take pride in the personalised approach we bring to every appointment. Before any treatment begins, we take the time to truly understand your skin, your concerns, and your goals. Because results that last start with a plan that fits you — not a one-size-fits-all solution.

Our clients travel from Bovenkarspel, Enkhuizen, Hoorn and throughout North Holland to experience the MT difference. We'd love to welcome you too.
